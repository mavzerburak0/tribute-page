Nikola Tesla

A tribute to an extraordinary man

Here's a timeline of Nikola Tesla's life:

  • 1859 - Nikola Tesla is born
  • 1870 - Tesla's brush with death - he had near-death circumstances after contracting cholera.
  • 1875 - Tesla enrolls in a Polytechnic based on a military scholarship.
  • 1880 - Tesla moves to prague to study philosophy.
  • 1881 - Tesla begins his career as chief electrical engineer.
  • 1882 - Tesla starts working for Edison in Paris with a company referred to as the Continental Edison Company.
  • 1885 - Tesla submits some of his first patents.
  • 1887 - Tesla re-imagines the electric motor, coming up with an induction motor that was operated by alternating current.
  • 1890 - Tesla begins his work on wireless power transmission.
  • 1891 - Tesla patents the Tesla Coil.
  • 1943 - Tesla died alone in a hotel room in New York.
